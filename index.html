VGC Damage Calculator

I have been working on a damage calculator tailor-made for the VGC community. It has been unofficially forked from the Trainer Tower calculator (which itself is an unofficial fork of the Nugget Bridge calculator), which I helped with maintenance on in 2020. Due to the discontinuation of Trainer Tower, it is now part of Nimbasa City Post.
